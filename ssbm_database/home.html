Welcome to the Super Smash Bros. Melee Database

Super Smash Bros. Melee is the sequel to the surprise 1999 hit, Super Smash Bros. for the Nintendo 64. Published in 2001, the game is the best-selling release for the Nintendo GameCube, with approximately 7.09 million copies sold. Many years since its release, the game still has a thriving competitive community. However, this site will not go deep into the competitive scene; it will merely be focusing on the content of the game. Included here is information regarding the characters, stages, and more.
