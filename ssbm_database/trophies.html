Trophies

Trophies are collectible items found throughout the game. While all 26 playable characters have 3 trophies, each earned from completing a Regular Match 1P mode, trophies may also represent a wide variety of characters, objects, and locations from Nintendo's history.

Obtaining Trophies

Regular Gameplay

Trophies can be found while playing a Regular Match 1P mode. In Classic Mode, trophies can be obtained during the "Snag the Trophies" minigame. In Adventure Mode, trophies have a chance of spawning on an adventure stage. In All-Star Mode, trophies can appear in the rest area in between matches.


Lottery

As time is spent playing the game, players will earn coins that can be used in the Lottery. When coins are spent here, players have a chance at obtaining a new trophy. As more trophies are earned, the chance of winning a new one decreases. Not all trophies can be earned from the Lottery; many require specific tasks to be completed.
